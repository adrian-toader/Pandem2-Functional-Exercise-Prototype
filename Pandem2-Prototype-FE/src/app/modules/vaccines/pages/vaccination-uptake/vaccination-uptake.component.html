Safe and effective vaccines are a powerful tool for ensuring public health and controlling the pandemic. Results from observational studies carried out to date have shown that the vaccines authorised in the EU/EEA are currently highly protective against severe avian influenza, hospitalisation, and death. The European Medicines Agency (EMA) evaluates vaccines before they are authorised for use in the EU by the European Commission.
